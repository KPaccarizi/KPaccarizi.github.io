Kosovo is blessed with beautiful mountains and waterfalls ranges that offer stunning landscapes and outdoor recreational opportunities

Shar Mountain (Maja e Sharrit)

Peak : Titov Vrv
Location : Kosovo-District of Prizren
Elevation : 2,652 m

Is the highest and most prominent mountain range in the country. It forms a natural border between Kosovo and North Macedonia, and its highest peak, Bistra. Shar Mountain is known for its picturesque alpine scenery, hiking trails, and winter sports activities.

Accursed Mountains (Bjeshkët e Nemuna)

Peak : Maja Jezercë
Location : Southern Kosovo
Elevation : 2,694 m

Also known as the Albanian Alps, extend across the northern border of Kosovo. This rugged and wild mountain range offers breathtaking landscapes, deep gorges, glacial lakes, and towering peaks. Hiking and mountaineering enthusiasts flock for its challenging trails and untouched natural beauty.

The Junik Mountains

Peak : Hajla
Location : Junik, western part of Kosovo
Elevation : 2,403 m

The region is known for its pristine landscapes and untouched nature, making it a haven for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. The Junik Mountains offer fantastic opportunities for hiking, trekking, and mountaineering.

Radavc Waterfall

Watercourse : White Drini
Location : Village of Radavc
Elevation : 25 m

The Radavc Waterfall is formed by the White Drin River as it cascades down a series of rocky steps, creating a mesmerizing display of rushing water and mist. The waterfall is particularly impressive during the spring when the melting snow and increased water flow enhance its grandeur

Mirusha Waterfalls

Watercourse : Mirusha River
Location : Malisheve
Elevation : 22m

Waterfalls are a series of cascading falls nestled in a scenic canyon. Surrounded by stunning rock formations and turquoise-colored pools, creating a picturesque and serene environment. Mirusha Waterfalls offer opportunities for swimming, picnicking, and hiking.

Shala Bajgores

Area : 9.3mi
Location : Decan
Elevation : 800m

Shala e Bajgores or Bajgora Canyon,is renowned for its pristine beauty and untouched landscapes. The canyon offers a serene and peaceful environment, surrounded by lush vegetation and rugged rock formations.
