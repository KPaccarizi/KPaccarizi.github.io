Kosovo boasts breathtaking mountains, waterfalls, and outdoor adventures

Sharr Mountain (Maja e Sharrit)

Peak : Titov Vrv
Location : Kosovo-District of Prizren
Elevation : 2,652 m

Shar Mountain, the highest range in Kosovo, borders North Macedonia and is famous for alpine beauty, hiking, and winter sports

Bjeshkët e Nemuna

Peak : Maja Jezercë
Location : Southern Kosovo
Elevation : 2,694 m

"The Albanian Alps, bordering Kosovo's north, provide stunning landscapes, gorges, lakes, and challenging hiking routes

The Junik Mountains

Peak : Hajla
Location : Junik, western part of Kosovo
Elevation : 2,403 m

The Junik Mountains, known for their pristine nature, are a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering great hiking and trekking

Radavc Waterfall

Watercourse : White Drini
Location : Village of Radavc
Elevation : 25 m

Radavc Waterfall, created by the White Drin River, offers a mesmerizing display, especially in spring with increased water flow

Mirusha Waterfalls

Watercourse : Mirusha River
Location : Malisheve
Elevation : 22m

Mirusha Waterfalls, nestled in a scenic canyon with turquoise pools, provide a picturesque environment for swimming and hiking

Shala of Bajgora

Area : 9.3mi
Location : Decan
Elevation : 800m

Shala of Bajgora or Bajgora Canyon, known for pristine beauty, offers a serene environment with lush vegetation and rugged rock formations
